Charlie Chaplin Profile

“Life is a beautiful magnificent thing, even to a jellyfish.” ― Charles Chaplin

  • Name: Charlie Chaplin
  • DOB: April 26, 1889
  • Profession: Filmaker/Cultural Icon
  • Description: Filmaker, crowd pleaser

  • Chaplin wrote, directed, produced, edited, scored, and starred in most of his films. He was a perfectionist, and his financial independence meant he often spent years on the development and production of a picture. His films are characterised by slapstick combined with pathos, and often feature the Tramp struggling against adversity.
